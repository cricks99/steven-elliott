The American Dream in Action

Steven Elliott's story embodies the American Dream. Born to a Canadian construction worker father and American secretary mother, Steven learned early that success comes through hard work and determination.

Early Life & Service

From his first job at age 12 to his service in the United States Marine Corps, Steven has always understood the value of hard work and dedication. His four years of military service instilled in him the values of honor, courage, and commitment that guide him today.

Business Leadership

After his military service, Steven became a successful entrepreneur, founding and operating multiple businesses including Rochester Laser Center and American Pride Tattoo Studio. His businesses have created jobs and contributed to Michigan's economy, proving that the American Dream is achievable through determination and free enterprise.

Fighting for Michigan

Steven has seen the American Dream become increasingly out of reach for too many families due to rising inflation, increasing crime, poor education, erosion of freedoms, economic stagnation, high taxes and burdensome regulations. He's running for Congress to fight back for working families and restore opportunity for all Americans.

Family & Community

Steven is actively involved in local events, charities, and organizations, giving back to the community that helped shape his success. He understands that strong families and strong communities are the backbone of America.
