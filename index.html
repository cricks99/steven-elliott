About Steven Elliott

A Proven Leader with Michigan Values

Steven Elliott's story is the American Dream in action. Born to a Canadian construction worker father and American secretary mother, Steven learned early that success comes through hard work and determination.

From his first job at age 12 to his service in the Marine Corps and his success as a business owner, Steven has lived the values he wants to bring to Congress: service, integrity, and an unwavering belief in America's potential.
