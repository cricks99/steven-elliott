National Issues

01

Economic Growth & Prosperity

Rebuilding the American Dream

Every American should have the opportunity to live their own American Dream. Upward mobility can be better realized with a growing economy and more quality jobs.

Key Priorities:

  • Eliminate burdensome regulations and bureaucratic delays
  • Reduce tax burden on businesses and families
  • Help businesses compete in the global marketplace
  • Create pathways for economic advancement
02

Border Security & Immigration

Protecting American Sovereignty

In 2021, the United States saw more than 2 million illegal border crossings. Our national sovereignty and security are at stake.

Key Priorities:

  • Enhance funding for Border Patrol and Customs Enforcement
  • End dangerous "catch and release" policies
  • Stop drug dealers, terrorists, and human traffickers
  • Strengthen deterrents to illegal entry
03

Law & Order

Safe Communities for All

Violent crime has plagued our communities for too long. Citizens deserve to feel safe in their homes, at work, and in their daily activities.

Key Priorities:

  • Fund our police - don't defund them
  • Support law enforcement with proper resources
  • Ensure swift justice for violent criminals
  • Protect victims' rights and community safety
04

National Defense

Peace Through Strength

America can preserve peace through strength by ensuring we maintain the most capable military force on Earth.

Key Priorities:

  • Properly fund our military and veterans
  • Provide oversight to reduce ineffective spending
  • Support our troops and their families
  • Maintain technological superiority
05

Small Business & Entrepreneurship

Empowering Innovation

Lower barriers to entry for new entrepreneurs so anyone with a good idea and hard work can start a business, grow, and thrive.

Key Priorities:

  • Reduce regulatory burden on small businesses
  • Support local job creation and economic development
  • Protect the rights of business owners
  • Encourage innovation and competition
06

Constitutional Rights & American Values

Defending Our Freedoms

Defend our Constitution, protect religious freedom, and preserve the values that make America exceptional.

Key Priorities:

  • Defend the First and Second Amendments
  • Protect religious liberty for all Americans
  • Support traditional family values
  • Preserve American sovereignty and independence
