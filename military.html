Answering the Call to Serve

Two weeks after his high school graduation, Steven joined the United States Marine Corps, just three days after his 18th birthday.

His service in the Marines changed his life forever, and for the better. Steven served 4 years in the Marine Corps as a Logistics Specialist at Camp Pendleton in Southern California.

Marine Corps Values

Steven's military service instilled core values that continue to guide him today:

Honor: Doing what's right, even when no one is watching
Courage: Standing up for what you believe in, regardless of the consequences
Commitment: Dedication to mission accomplishment and taking care of Marines and their families

These values shaped Steven's understanding of service before self - a principle he carries into his business endeavors and his campaign for Congress.

Defending America's Borders

Steven's deployment to the Southern Border gave him firsthand experience with the challenges facing America's border security. He witnessed the threats posed by drug cartels, human traffickers, and illegal crossings - experiences that inform his strong stance on border security today.

This service reinforced his belief that a nation must control its borders to maintain sovereignty and protect its citizens.
