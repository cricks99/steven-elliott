Love of Country

Steven is not only a veteran and businessman, he is a patriot who loves his country and believes that America is exceptional.

His experiences - from childhood struggles to military service to building successful businesses - have given him a deep appreciation for the opportunities that America provides to those willing to work hard and pursue their dreams.

The Decision to Run

It is for these reasons that Steven has decided to run for United States Congress to challenge the establishment and fight for hard working Americans.

Steven believes that his unique combination of experiences - growing up in a working-class family, serving his country in the Marines, and building successful businesses - gives him the perspective needed to represent Michigan's 12th Congressional District effectively.

Fighting for America's Future

Steven's campaign is about more than politics - it's about preserving the American Dream for future generations. He believes that America's best days are ahead if we return to the principles that made our nation great:

Individual Liberty: Protecting the rights guaranteed by our Constitution
Free Enterprise: Allowing businesses to grow and create jobs without excessive government interference
Personal Responsibility: Encouraging self-reliance and hard work
Strong Defense: Maintaining the world's strongest military to preserve peace
Secure Borders: Protecting American sovereignty and safety

A Proven Track Record

Steven's life story demonstrates his ability to succeed in challenging circumstances. From working multiple jobs as a teenager to serving his country to building successful businesses, he has consistently delivered results.

Now he's ready to bring that same dedication and work ethic to Congress, fighting tirelessly for the families of Michigan's 12th Congressional District.
